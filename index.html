Emotional Support Animals

An emotional support animal is an animal that helps individuals who have a psychiatric disability. These animals provide companionship; however they are not registered service animals"

Service Dogs

A service dog is an animal who is trained to do perform task for people who have disabilities. These can vary from guide dogs to dogs who alert people when they're about to have a diabetic attack.

Pets

Pets are animals people can choose to bring home and care for; you can obtain a a pet by going to a shelter or picking one up off the street. A pet can range from something as simple as a dog or cat to something like a tiger. (Don't own a tiger, just get a cat)
